Features

Astroguide (DB)

The site lists all aqstromon currently available in the game, which includes their basic information and gem stats.

Sorting & Filtering

You can sort and filter the astromon chart by name, nat, element, type of combat, skills and statistics, just click on the corresponding heading or type below it.

Astromon Details

Clicking "Details" below the astromon icon will take you to a page with detailed data such as base stats, skills, tier list and stats with equipped gems.

Details: Build tab

The numbers are derived from the maximum stats of the astromon at level 60 or superevo and the improvements coming from the gems 6* +15:

  • HP: Total health after gems: base_HP + (base_HP * (equiped gems + gem set)).
  • DEF: Total Defense after gems: base_DEF + (base_HP * (equiped gems + gem set)).
  • dmg block: Damage mitigation, after gems: Total DEF / (Total DEF + 1200).
  • eHP: Effective Healt is the term commonly used in games to describe how 'tanky' a particular actor/hero/monster is. Which is basically the actual health a monster have multiplied by all damage reduction effects that monster have active at the time. A simple way of explaining what is EHP can be taken from this example, a monster that has 100k damage will need, quite literally 5 hits to kill a monster with 500k EHP, it does not matter what buff or defenses the target monster has, because it's already included in it's EHP calculation; which differs when we talk about HP, a monster with 10k damage doesn't necessarily mean that the mon needs five hits to kill a 50k HP monster, that highly depends on what damage reduction effects the target monster has at the time, from defense values to buffs. EHP is the direct numerical representation of how 'tanky' a monster is when it comes to what it takes to kill it.: Total HP / (1 – dmg block).
  • ATK: Average damage (100%) done by the astromon, does NOT include factors such as astromon's element advantage, passive skills and the 5.5 attack multiplier: base_ATK + (base_ATK * (equiped gems + gem set)).
  • CS Dmg: Total Damage Multiplier (depends on enemie max HP) after gems: Total ATK * 6.
  • US Dmg: Total Damage Multiplier (depends on enemie max HP) after gems: Total ATK * 4.
  • HP Aggr Dmg: Total Damage Multiplier after gems (depends on own total HP): Total HP * 0.3.
  • DEF Aggr Dmg: Total Damage Multiplier after gems (depends on own total DEF): Total DEF * 4.
  • Crit Dmg: Total Critical Damage Multiplier.
  • Crit Rate: Probability of performing a critical attack.
  • DMG on Crit: Damage this astromon could deal whenever it crits. after gems: (CD * total ATK) + total ATK.
  • Resist: Probability of resisting the effect of an enemy ability, 85% is the maximum resistance that an astromon can have all excess is ignored (90% => 85%)

    Calculadora de estadísticas con gemas

    Los números se derivan de las estadísticas maximas del astromon en nivel 60 o superevo y las mejoras provenientes de gemas porcentuales 6* +15:

  • HP: Vida total, proveniente de las estadísticas base del astromon + gemas.
  • DEF: Defensa total, proveniente de las estadísticas base del astromon + gemas.
  • dmg block: Reducción de daño, se obtiene con la formula: defensa total / (defensa total + 1200).
  • eHP: Es el termino usado en juegos para describir que tan “tanque” es un personaje, cantidad de daño que se necesita para matarlo. Que es básicamente la salud real que tiene un monstruo, multiplicado por todos los efectos de reducción de daño que el monstruo tiene activo en ese momento. Se obtiene de la formula: Hp total / (1 – dmg block).
  • ATK: Daño potencial hecho por el astromon, NO incluye factores como ventaja de elemento del astromon,multiplicador 5.5 de ataque de las animaciones ni pasivas de habilidades execpto por:
  • CS: Daño Total hecho por la habilidad "Ataque valiente": Total ATK * 6.
  • US: Daño total hecho por la habilidad "Golpe inescapable ": Total ATK * 4.
  • HP Aggr: Daño total (depende de la vida total propia): Total HP * 0.3.
  • DEF Aggr: Daño total (depende de la defensa total propia): Total DEF * 4.
  • Crit Dmg: Multiplicador de daño que se aplica cuando se realiza un golpe crítico proveniente de las estadísticas base del astromon + gemas.
  • Crit Rate: Probabilidad de realizar un ataque crítico, proveniente de las estadísticas base del astromon + gemas.
  • DMG on Crit: Daño total teniendo en cuenta el multiplicador total de daño crítico: ATK total * CD.
  • Resist: Probabilidad de resistir el efecto de una habilidad enemiga, 85% es la maxima probabilidad que se puede obtener todo el exceso es ignorado (90% => 85%), proveniente de las estadísticas base del astromon y gemas.
